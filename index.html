Detroit Roses

Detroit Roses is a Detroit-based non-profit organization geared towards showcasing the beauty of Detroit through meaningful volunteer service, impactful events, and fashionable apparel.

Find Out More

What is Detroit Roses?

Detroit Roses is a community revitalization and quality clothing brand. Our vision is to showcase the beauty of Detroit through community involvement and quality products that represent the culture, creativity and strength of our great city.
